Instructions

    1. Questions

  • Questions be like "Name an alibi for skipping school"
  • Answers be like "Being ill"

    2. Start Round

  • One each to the podium
  • Answer is read - Hit buzzer to give an answer
  • Then contestant gets to answer
  • Whoever gets more points can choose to play round with the team

    3. Play Round

  • Team members get two answer in turns
  • Strike when answer not on the board
  • If three strikes other team can steal
  • If other team gets one answer on the board they get all the points
